    Brief Introduction To Guizhou Province

    Guizhou Province, referred to as "Qian" or "Gui", is located in the hinterland of Southwest China, bordering Chongqing, Sichuan, Hunan, Yunnan and Guangxi. It is the transportation hub of Southwest china. The world-famous mountain tourism destination and mountain tourism province, the country's first national large data comprehensive test area, the national ecological civilization test area, inland open economy pilot area. Under the jurisdiction of Guiyang City, Zunyi City, Liupanshui City, Anshun City, Bijie City, Tongren City, Qianxinan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Qiandongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture, Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous prefecture..

    Miao paper-cut

    Qiandongnan Miao said "flowers", "paper-cut for the flower seedling paper". In the long-term practice of the art of embroidery, the paper-cut patterns instead of drawing a pattern in the embroidery surface, can accurately reproduce, save labor and time, but also can keep the surface clean and bright embroidered, since then, they put paper-cut patterns as the "blue embroidery art". In order to ensure the quality of embroidery, the Miao women put their intelligence, talent and emotions into the paper-cut patterns, making them different, with distinctive national characteristics.
