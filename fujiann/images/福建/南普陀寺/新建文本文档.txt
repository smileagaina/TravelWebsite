

Nanpu is a well-known buddhist temple in minnan and the country. The whole temple buildings nestled below show strange peaks, the famous buddhist college is located in the south of fujian province, and xiamen university only of one wall lie between, but there are two kinds of artistic conception: "ancient and modern, the young monk and college students neighbors". The temple was built in the Ming dynasty (1403-1424) and was destroyed in the Ming dynasty (1628). In 1684, emperor kangxi was rebuilt. Because the temple is the goddess of guanyin, it is also located in the south of zhoushan putuo, one of the four buddhist temples in China.
