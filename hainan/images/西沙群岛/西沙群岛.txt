

Xisha islands is mainly composed of yongle and xuande islands, this piece of large and small coral islands floating in more than 50 square kilometers of sea, beautiful and pure. The islands' first big island, yongxing island, is like a tropical botanical garden. There are tropical plants, and the seasons are luxuriant; The island is known as the "bird's paradise", with more than 60,000 seabirds on the island. As far away from the mainland, the beaches are white, the water is clean, the highest visibility is 40 meters, and the seven lantau is the best dive site.
