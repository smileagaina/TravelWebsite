

Lushan mountain is located in jiujiang in jiangxi province in central China, south of the Yangtze river in the north, the east lake, covers an area of 302 square kilometers, the whole of a total of more than 90 peaks, peak hanyang peak, 1473.4 meters above sea level. Among the peaks, there are many gullies, caves, waterfalls and streams, and the terrain is complex.

Mt lushan appeared numerous times in the poems and songs of the literati, which was the most famous of all the poems of su shi. Now the lushan mountain is a very mature tourism scenic area, castle peak green water, waterfall, in the mountain the sunrise, and modern villa, attracted many visitors come to a snapshot of "looks like".
