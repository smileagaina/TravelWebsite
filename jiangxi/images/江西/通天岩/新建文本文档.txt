

Tongtian rock is a treasure-house of ancient grottoes in the south of China, and there are some of the largest stone shrines in jiangxi, with 279 stone shrines and 359 stone sculptures. The statues were made in the tang and song dynasties. These kinds of images are different, delicate and beautiful, and they are the treasure of ancient stone carving.
